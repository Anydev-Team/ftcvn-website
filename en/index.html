FIRST® Tech Challenge là gì?

FIRST Tech Challenge is more than just building robots. Teams (up to 15 team members, grades 7-12) participate in the challenge to design, build, program, and operate robots to compete in leagues. The robot kits are reusable from year to year and can be programmed in various levels of the Java language. In addition to designing and building robots, teams also participate in fundraising, branding, communications, and community outreach to win various prizes.

Guided by coaches and mentors, students can develop STEM skills and practice mechanical design principles, while also embracing values such as hard work, creativity, and teamwork. With Gracious Professionalism®, students are encouraged to create their team's identity and become ambassadors for FIRST and STEM in their own communities.

Each season will conclude with regional tournaments and the FIRST Championship in the United States.
